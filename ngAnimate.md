# ngAnimate
### 我们可以使用CSS3的方式添加动画
- ng-enter
- ng-enter-active
- ng-leave
- ng-leave-active
### 支持的指令
  if,view,repeat,include,swtich,repeat
- ng-enter-stagger
- animation-delay

## 支持的指令
class,show,hide,model等
- ng-hide-add
- ng-hide-add-active
- ng-hide-remove
- ng-hide-remove-active

## JS方式
animation()
- enter/leave
- removeClass/addClass
